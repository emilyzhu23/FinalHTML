Slopes?

  • Now there are of course various types of slopes, basically the snow you ski down, depending on your skill level.
  • If you're a beginner, it's likely you'll start out on the bunny slopes. These are easier to navigate and approachable because they're generally less steep and pretty short.
  • As your skill level increases, you'll be able to go for longer and steeper runs where you'll be able to go faster .
